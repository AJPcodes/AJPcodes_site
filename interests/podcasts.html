Podcasts

Probably my favorite forms of informative entertainment.

I started listening to podcasts while I was living in Japan. Listening kept me connected to American (and a bit of British) culture. I could pop in my headphones and be right back in the states. Podcasts helped me run a marathon, and I all but refuse to do housework without radio now.
